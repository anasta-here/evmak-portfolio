About

Evgenii is a portrait, lifestyle, travel, and interior photographer whose work is calming, delightful, joyous and colour-driven. His photos tell a story. When he is not behind the camera, he can be found adventuring with his young daughter and his scruffy dog, hunting for the next cup of coffee, perfecting his interior design skills, and plotting his next bike trip. His all-nature studio is located in a countryside village not far from Izhevsk, Russia.
